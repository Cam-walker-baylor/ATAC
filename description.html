üî¨ Key Biological Findings

  • 1,646 transcription factors analyzed across the genome
  • 630 TFs show decreased binding (38.3%) after TAK-981
  • 697 TFs show increased binding (42.3%) after TAK-981
  • AP-1 family most affected: JUNB, FOS, FOSL1 dramatically decreased
  • Effect range: -18% to +43% binding change
  • Statistical significance: All changes p < 1e-169
